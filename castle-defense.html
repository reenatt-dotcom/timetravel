Castle Defense Strategies

Castle defense was a critical aspect of medieval warfare. Castles were designed to withstand sieges and protect their inhabitants. Here are some key strategies used in castle defense:

  • Moats and Drawbridges
  • Battlements and Merlons
  • Portcullis and Gatehouses
  • Barbicans and Murder Holes
  • Siege Towers and Battering Rams
  • Walls and Towers
