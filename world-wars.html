World Wars

The World Wars refer to two major global conflicts that occurred in the 20th century. World War I (1914-1918) and World War II (1939-1945) had a profound impact on the world, resulting in significant social, political, and economic changes.

Main Events and Consequences

  • Assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand
  • Treaty of Versailles
  • Rise of totalitarian regimes
  • Atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki
  • Creation of the United Nations
